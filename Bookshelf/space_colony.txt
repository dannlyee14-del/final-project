In the year 2235, humanity had expanded beyond Earth.

Large colonies existed on Mars, the Moon, and several orbital stations.

The most advanced settlement was known as Nova Colony.

Located on a distant planet orbiting the star Kepler-186, Nova Colony was home to over fifty thousand people.

Scientists, engineers, farmers, and explorers worked together to build a new future among the stars.

Among the residents lived a sixteen-year-old student named Ethan.

Unlike most teenagers, Ethan dreamed of becoming a deep-space explorer.

Every evening he visited the colony's observation dome.

From there he could see distant stars shining across the darkness.

One night, the colony received a strange transmission.

.... . .-.. .--.

The communication center immediately became active.

Engineers rushed to their stations.

Computers began analyzing the signal.

Ethan recognized the pattern.

It was Morse code.

The message translated to:

HELP

Nobody knew where the transmission originated.

The colony council formed an investigation team.

Because Ethan had extensive knowledge of communication systems, he was invited to assist.

The signal appeared to originate from an abandoned mining station several hundred kilometers away.

The next morning, a rover team departed.

The journey took several hours.

As they crossed the rocky landscape, they encountered massive cliffs, frozen lakes, and unusual crystal formations.

Eventually they reached the abandoned station.

The facility appeared deserted.

Yet the signal continued.

.... . .-.. .--.

Inside the station, dust covered every surface.

Old machinery stood silent.

Broken monitors lined the walls.

But one terminal remained active.

A message appeared on the screen.

?12*12?

Ethan solved it immediately.

The terminal unlocked.

A hidden door opened.

.fig
      ____________
     | TERMINAL   |
     |____________|

Beyond the door was a secret research laboratory.

The room contained advanced technology unlike anything currently used in the colony.

At the center stood an artificial intelligence core.

The AI had been operating alone for decades.

Its power supply was nearly exhausted.

The HELP signal had been an automated distress call.

.fig
          _______
         /  AI   \
        /  CORE   \
        ----------
.figend

The AI explained that it had been created by the colony's original founders.

Its purpose was to preserve historical records and scientific discoveries.

If the system failed, valuable knowledge would be lost forever.

To restore the AI, Ethan needed to solve several security challenges.

The first challenge appeared.

?sqrt(225)?

The second challenge followed.

?power(2,7)?

The final challenge appeared moments later.

?1000/25?

.fig
        [RESTORING]
        ████░░░░░░
.figend

As each answer was processed, the AI's systems gradually recovered.

Lights throughout the laboratory illuminated.

Old servers restarted.

Historical archives became accessible once more.

Finally, the restoration reached one hundred percent.

.fig
        [RESTORING]
        ██████████
.figend

The AI thanked Ethan and his team.

It then revealed a hidden archive containing the earliest records of Nova Colony.

The files described the struggles faced by the first settlers.

Limited resources.

Harsh weather.

Equipment failures.

Despite these challenges, they never gave up.

Their determination allowed future generations to thrive.

Upon returning home, Ethan shared the discoveries with the colony council.

The historical records were added to the public library.

Citizens gained a deeper appreciation for the sacrifices made by the colony's founders.

Ethan became known throughout Nova Colony as the student who saved its history.

Years later, Ethan achieved his dream of becoming a deep-space explorer.

Before departing on his first mission, he visited the observation dome one final time.

Looking toward the stars, he remembered the mysterious signal that started everything.

.... . .-.. .--.

HELP.

A simple message.

A message that saved an entire chapter of human history.
